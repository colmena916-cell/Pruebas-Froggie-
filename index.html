About Froggie AI

Froggie AI was born out of a clear need: to rescue the true essence of roleplay and interactive creative writing. On an internet full of commercial platforms that limit your imagination with rigid filters and memories that forget your story within a few paragraphs, this space proposes the opposite.

This is a handmade project, focused on bringing to life deep psychological profiles, text rich in narrative, and absolute freedom for your stories. Here, you have control over the aesthetics and the direction of your characters.

Support the Project

Froggie AI is built entirely from scratch, out of passion and a deep love for free storytelling. If you love this space and want to help keep it alive, independent, and growing, your support makes a world of difference. Every donation directly funds the servers that make these minds run.

Age Requirement: This platform allows for uncensored and mature narrative development. By registering, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old (or the legal age of majority in your jurisdiction).

Content & Ownership: You retain full ownership and intellectual property of any original characters, prompts, and lore you create. Froggie AI does not claim any rights over your creative writing.

Liability Disclaimer: The AI models generate responses unpredictably. Froggie AI is not responsible for the content generated by the bots. Furthermore, users agree not to use the platform to generate real-world hate speech, harassment, or illegal activities.

Your data and stories will not be commercialized or shared with external companies. Respect for the community's privacy and creative freedom are the fundamental pillars of this space.
